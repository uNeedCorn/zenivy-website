# About

## 方向敘述 | Direction
Zenivy 的方向很單純：為清晰保留條件，為生成保留時間。  
Zen 是「能看見、能選擇」的狀態；Ivy 是「能延續、能累積」的變化。  
我們不追求更多，而是維持留白——讓重要的事有空間發生。

Zenivy is simple in direction: preserve the conditions for clarity, and the time for growth to continue.  
Zen is a state where seeing and choosing are possible. Ivy is change that accumulates and lasts.  
We don’t aim for more—we maintain room, so what matters can happen.

---

## 核心原則 | Principles
1. **留白優先**：保留空間，比增加內容更重要。  
2. **避免雜訊**：降低打斷與稀釋，讓清晰成為可能。  
3. **選擇清楚**：讓人更容易看見重點，做出自己的選擇。  
4. **可持續**：改變必須能長期維持，能累積，而不靠短期衝刺。  
5. **工具退場**：好的系統會退到背景，讓生活站到前景。

1. **Room first**: preserving space matters more than adding content.  
2. **Avoid noise**: reduce interruption and dilution so clarity becomes possible.  
3. **Clear choice**: make it easier to see what matters and choose deliberately.  
4. **Sustainable**: change must last and accumulate, not depend on short bursts.  
5. **Tools step back**: good systems fade into the background so life can come forward.

---

## 定義 | Definitions
Zen：清晰的狀態。  
Ivy：持續的生成。  

Zen: a state of clarity.  
Ivy: continuity of growth.
