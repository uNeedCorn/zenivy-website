# Zenivy Manifesto

## WHY / Tagline
為內在秩序留白。  
Make room for inner order.

---

## 核心信念 | Core Belief
我們相信，生活需要一種能自我生成的秩序。當一切被填滿，清晰就不再是預設，而成為稀缺。  
Zenivy 的存在，是為了維持留白：避免雜訊，讓清晰與自洽得以出現，並能長久維持。

We believe life needs room for order to emerge on its own. When everything is filled, clarity stops being the default and becomes scarce.  
Zenivy exists to maintain room: avoid noise, so clarity and coherence can emerge—and sustain over time.

---

## 方向敘述 | Direction
Zenivy 的方向很單純：為清晰保留條件，為生成保留時間。  
Zen 是「能看見、能選擇」的狀態；Ivy 是「能延續、能累積」的變化。  
我們不追求更多，而是維持留白——讓重要的事有空間發生。

Zenivy is simple in direction: preserve the conditions for clarity, and the time for growth to continue.  
Zen is a state where seeing and choosing are possible. Ivy is change that accumulates and lasts.  
We don’t aim for more—we maintain room, so what matters can happen.

---

## 雜訊的定義 | What “Noise” Means Here
雜訊不是「資訊」，而是那些佔據心智、打斷連貫、稀釋重要性的輸入。  
Zenivy 追求的不是隔絕世界，而是避免雜訊，讓輸入回到有邊界、有節奏、可被消化。

Noise is not information—it’s input that occupies the mind, breaks coherence, and dilutes what matters.  
Zenivy doesn’t seek isolation from the world; it avoids noise so input can have boundaries, rhythm, and be digestible.

---

## 核心原則 | Principles
1. **留白優先**：保留空間，比增加內容更重要。  
2. **避免雜訊**：降低打斷與稀釋，讓清晰成為可能。  
3. **選擇清楚**：讓人更容易看見重點，做出自己的選擇。  
4. **可持續**：改變必須能長期維持，能累積，而不靠短期衝刺。  
5. **工具退場**：好的系統會退到背景，讓生活站到前景。

1. **Room first**: preserving space matters more than adding content.  
2. **Avoid noise**: reduce interruption and dilution so clarity becomes possible.  
3. **Clear choice**: make it easier to see what matters and choose deliberately.  
4. **Sustainable**: change must last and accumulate, not depend on short bursts.  
5. **Tools step back**: good systems fade into the background so life can come forward.

---

## 使命與願景 | Mission & Vision
**使命 Mission**  
以可持續的方式維持留白，避免雜訊，讓清晰與自洽得以長久。  
Sustain room, avoid noise, and keep clarity and coherence durable over time.

**願景 Vision**  
讓更多人的日常能自我生成秩序，讓重要的事有空間發生。  
A world where everyday life can generate its own order—so what matters has room to happen.

---

## 我們做什麼、也不做什麼 | What We Do / What We Don’t
**我們做 What we do**  
建立能維持留白的系統，讓清晰更容易發生，讓生成能延續。  
Build systems that maintain room—so clarity emerges more easily and growth can continue.

**我們不做 What we don’t**  
不追求更多刺激、不追求填滿、不追求讓人永遠在線。  
We don’t pursue more stimulation, more filling, or keeping people perpetually engaged.
