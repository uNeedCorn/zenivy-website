Privacy Policy

Zenivy (developed by Nextora) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Service Description

Inboxless is an email analysis and inbox cleanup tool that helps you organize and manage your Gmail messages.

Data We Collect

When you use our service, we collect the following data through Google APIs:

From Google OAuth

  • Email Address: To identify your account.
  • Display Name: To personalize your experience.
  • Profile Picture: To display in the application interface.

From Gmail API

  • Email Headers: Sender address, subject line, and timestamp.
  • Email Labels: To understand email categorization.
  • Message IDs: To perform delete/archive actions when you request them.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT read or store the body content of your emails.
  • We do NOT access your email attachments.
  • We do NOT store any email data on our servers.

OAuth Permissions

Our application requests the following Google OAuth permissions:

  • gmail.readonly: To read your emails for inbox analysis.
  • gmail.modify: To delete or archive emails (only when you initiate the action).

How We Use Google User Data

We access Google user data solely to provide the core functionality of our email analysis service. Here is exactly how we use the data:

Data Processing

  • Email Metadata Analysis: We read email headers (sender, subject, date) to categorize your inbox into newsletters, notifications, and personal emails.
  • Sender Statistics: We aggregate sender information to show you who sends you the most emails.
  • Cleanup Actions: When you choose to delete or archive emails, we execute those actions on your behalf through the Gmail API.

Data Handling Practices

  • Client-Side Processing: All email analysis is performed locally in your browser or desktop application. We do not transfer your email content to our servers.
  • No Persistent Storage: Email data is held in memory only during your active session and is cleared when you close the application.
  • No Secondary Use: We do not use your Google user data for advertising, marketing, or any purpose unrelated to the core email analysis functionality.
  • No Data Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or share your Google user data with any third parties.
  • No AI/ML Training: Your Google user data is not used to train artificial intelligence or machine learning models.

Google API Services Limited Use Disclosure

Zenivy's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth: For secure authentication.
  • Supabase: For user identity management.

Data Retention

We follow a minimal data retention policy. Email data used for analysis is stored only on your device and is cleared when you close the application. Account-related data will be removed when you delete your account.

Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encrypted transmission (HTTPS) and secure authentication mechanisms.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke the application's access to your Google account at any time.
  • Request deletion of your account and related data.
  • Inquire about how we handle your data.

Policy Updates

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be announced on our website or via email (if you have provided contact information).

Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, please email us at hello@zenivy.app.
