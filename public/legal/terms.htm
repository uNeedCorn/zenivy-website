4. Inboxless Service Terms

The following terms apply specifically to the Inboxless email analysis and cleanup service.

4.1 Service Features

Inboxless helps you:

  • Analyze your Gmail inbox to understand email sources and patterns
  • Identify newsletters, notifications, and bulk senders
  • Clean up unwanted emails through batch deletion
  • Unsubscribe from mailing lists with one click

4.2 Gmail Access Permission

Inboxless requires the gmail.modify permission to function. This permission allows Inboxless to:

  • Read your email messages and metadata
  • Delete emails when you explicitly request it
  • Modify email labels (trash, archive)
Your Control: Inboxless will never automatically delete or modify your emails. All destructive actions require your explicit confirmation.

4.3 Irreversible Actions Warning

WARNING: Deleting emails is permanent and irreversible.

Once emails are deleted, they cannot be recovered. Before performing delete operations:

  • Review the selected emails carefully
  • Consider using "Archive" instead of "Delete" first
  • Back up important emails before cleanup
  • Start with a small batch to test

4.4 Service Limitations

Please be aware of the following limitations:

  • Gmail Only: Inboxless currently supports Gmail accounts only
  • API Limits: Gmail API rate limits may affect processing speed for large mailboxes
  • Analysis Accuracy: Email categorization is based on heuristics and may not be 100% accurate
  • Desktop App: Inboxless is a desktop application (macOS); web version is for development testing only

4.5 Data Handling

Inboxless processes your email data locally on your device. Your emails are:

  • Never uploaded to our servers
  • Never stored in any database
  • Immediately discarded when you close the application

For complete details, see the Inboxless Privacy Policy section.
